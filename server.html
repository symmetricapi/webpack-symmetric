iOS Instructions

  1. Click the certificate above to begin installation.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions for installing the cert under your system profiles.
  3. After installation go to Settings -> About -> Certificate Trust Settings and enable Symmetric Proxy CA.

Android Instructions

  1. Click the certificate above to download.
  2. Enter your pin to begin installation.
  3. Android will install as "User credentials" with the name you provided but also as a "Trusted credentials".
  4. You can view "Trusted credentials" under Settings -> Security -> Credential storage -> Trusted credentials.
  5. The new entry under "User credentials" may be removed.

macOS Instructions

  1. Click the certificate above to download.
  2. Open the downloaded symmetric_ca.crt file and open it to install into your keychain.
  3. Within the Keychain Access app locate the Symmetric Proxy CA certificate.
  4. Open the Symmetric Proxy CA certificate and under Trust update the settings to "Always Trust".
